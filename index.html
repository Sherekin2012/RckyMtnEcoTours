What is Eco-Tourism?

Eco-tourism has become increasingly popular in recent years. It is a way for those who are environmentally conscious to experience local people, ecosystems, and wildlife with little disturbance to the natural environment. The goal is to preserve the natural beauty and wildness for generations to come.

So, Are Eco-Tours any Fun?

Of course, they are! Come ride the rapids or hike into the wild back country with us and we will show you! You may see creatures few get to see. And in the process, learn new techniques for a greener approach to living, and playing, in an earth friendly, sustainable, way. Everything we need is packed in and nothing is left behind. The goal? To leave the parks and wilderness areas cleaner than when we arrived.
